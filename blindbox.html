What's Hidden Inside the Blind Box?

Hello, everyone!

Currently, the blind box craze is going viral among young people, captivating hearts with the excitement of collecting beloved characters or completing sought-after series.

Admittedly, its appeal is understandable, ranging from the emotional thrill of unboxing a surprise to its social value in fueling conversations and connections among peers. However, the risks cannot be ignored. First, many get caught in a cycle of endlessly pursuing rare items, leading to impulsive spending or unnecessary waste. More critically, the very mechanism resembles gambling, which may cultivate addictive shopping habits over time.

So, what's truly hidden inside isn't just a toy, but an engineered marketing trick. Let's learn to seek joy wisely, without falling into marketing traps.

Thank you.
